Do's and Dont's


It is extremly important to know Do's and Dont's for patients under home quarantine,specially with this second wave everyone must be in accordance with them.Here are our major Do's and Dont's.


Do's

  • Stay in a well ventilated room.

  • Use a separate toilet.

  • Keep a distance from family members especially elderly people,pregnant ladies,children and people suffering from diseases.

  • Change and wash your mask regularly.

Dont's

  • Do not share your room with anyone.

  • Do not move across common rooms and areas.

  • Avoid sharing things like clothing,utensils,bedding,etc.

  • Do not reuse disposable mask.

Myths


If people begin to believe COVID myths, then they will not take the right action at the right time. If they test positive, instead of seeking a doctor’s advice or opting for hospitalization, they may want to try medicines or methods that can’t actually cure them. Valuable time will be wasted and lives could be lost.


Draw your information and knowledge from reputed sources. If you receive random COVID information in the form of messages from unverifiable sources, do not share or forward without confirming the news first. We all have to play a proactive part in preventing this menace.
